Delicious Pani Puri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups moong dal, split and skinned
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine moong dal, urad dal, turmeric, and salt. Add water and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the dal and grind to a smooth paste using minimal water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook for another minute.
  5. Add the ground dal paste and cook on low heat, stirring continuously until thick.
  6. Add lemon juice, chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with mint chutney.
